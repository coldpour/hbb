How to Help

Spread the Word

We want as many people as possible to hear about this trip in hopes of inspiring as many young women as possible. Like us on Facebook to show your support and tell your friends to do the same!

Donate Money

If you would like to financially support our voyage we would really appreciate it! All extra proceeds, after our $2,500 trip budget is met, go to YMCA Camp Menogyn to fund trips for future young women explorers. Click the button at the top of the page to donate securely via PayPal or contact us at hudsonbaybound@gmail.com to find out how to donate!

Mail checks payable to "Hudson Bay Bound" to:
Hudson Bay Bound
2915 Bruce CT.
Inver Grove Heights, MN 55077

Sponsor Our Trip

Want to see your company logo on our canoe or jackets as we paddle this route? It would be great advertisement.

Donate Gear

If you have any gear to donate for our trip we would love to use it. See The Trip page for information about what we still need.

Help with Media Coverage

This may be the most important way to help -- we want to reach as many young women as possible to inspire them to go on their own adventures. Get the word out there with the media!
